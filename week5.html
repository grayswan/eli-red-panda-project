Red pandas

About red pandas

Red Pandas live in trees.

Red pandas eat fruit.

Red pandas are very good climbers.

Red pandas are a member of the raccoon famliy.

Red pandas are 20-26 inches long.

Red pandas are very playful in captivity.

Red pandas are not common animals.

Red pandas are are generally quiet.

Red pandas are found in high altitude temperate forests with bamboo.
